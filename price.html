Price Sheet

If the Initial Reaction of the work is rated an 8 or above and the grade received is equal to or above a 3.7 the time spent and cost of materials are then justified. In this case the Cost of Materials can be added into the total sale price of the work and the Time Spent can be multiplied by $200.

In the case that the Initial Reaction of the work is rated above an 8 and the grade received is below a 3.7 the time spent and Cost of Materials are then partially justified. In this case the Cost of Materials can be halved and added into the total sale price of the work and the Time Spent can be multiplied by $100.

In the case that the Initial Reaction of the work is rated below an 8 and the grade received is equal to or above a 3.7 the time spent and Cost of Materials are then partially justified. In this case the Cost of Materials can be added into the total sale price of the work and the Time Spent can be multiplied by $15.

In the case that the Initial Reaction of the work is rated below an 8 and the grade received is below a 3.7 the time spent and Cost of Materials are not justified. In this case the Cost of Materials cannot be added into the total sale price of the work and the Time Spent can be multiplied by $2.22. (Based on the cost of tuition calculated at .037 a minute)
